Hair & Scalp Diseases

Alopecia Areata

Alopecia refers to hair loss that occurs when the immune system mistakenly attacks hair follicles, leading to hair loss on the scalp or other parts of the body.

Dandruff

Dandruff is a common scalp condition characterized by flakes of dead skin that appear as white or yellowish scales on the scalp and hair. It often results from the overgrowth of a yeast-like fungus called Malassezia, which thrives on the scalp's natural oils.

Baldness

Treatment options for baldness include medications like minoxidil or finasteride, hair transplant surgery, and lifestyle changes to promote scalp health. Baldness can impact self-esteem and quality of life, prompting individuals to seek cosmetic solutions..
